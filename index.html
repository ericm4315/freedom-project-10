What is it that companies and social media platforms do not do that makes cyberbullying so prevalent? Why with the lack of filtering offensive messages of course!

The Problems

Lack of focus from Social Media:

Most Social Media and/or online games with a community often don’t acknowledge the flaws in their algorithms to filter messages. Therefore, many people start saying bad things without any repercussions.

Use of work-arounds:

Many people when they want to make fun of someone or bring negativity onto the table, even if some platforms have some filtering algorithm that filters offensive content, they are not perfect, and it is very easy to work around the algorithm to send a negative message. Instagram, TikTok and even YouTube have this issue where messages can be filtered but there are many ways for people to get around this.

Filters often mess up.

YouTube especially has this problem with their comments and content in general. A lot of the time, many innocent channels get terminated because of their copyright system or community guidelines. And the actual bad things don’t get noticed at all.

Do it yourself.

Many social media platforms and games have no filters which means that it is up to the community to make it for them. A big one is Discord with server moderators having to hire a bot (Which might not work very well or may go offline) to filter the messages.
